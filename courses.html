Program 01

Diploma & Certificate in Computer Science

Our Computer Science programs provide a comprehensive foundation in computational thinking, software engineering, and applied mathematics. Students gain both theoretical knowledge and hands-on practical skills.

What You Will Learn:
  • Programming (Python, Java, C++)
  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Database Design & Management
  • Software Engineering Principles
  • Operating Systems & Computer Architecture
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning Basics

Admission Requirements:
  • Diploma: KCSE Mean Grade C (Plain) or equivalent
  • Certificate: KCSE Mean Grade D+ or equivalent
  • Strong aptitude for mathematics and logical reasoning

Note: All programs are offered under the Kenya National Qualifications Framework Level 5th standard. CO2 neutral campus operations.

Program 02

Diploma & Certificate in Information Technology

The IT program focuses on the practical administration and management of information systems and network infrastructure. Graduates are equipped for roles in system administration, networking, and cybersecurity.

Core Modules:
  • Computer Networking (CompTIA N+ aligned)
  • Cybersecurity Fundamentals
  • Systems Administration (Windows & Linux)
  • Cloud Computing Essentials
  • IT Project Management
  • Technical Support & Helpdesk Operations

Career Paths:
  1. Network Administrator
  2. Cybersecurity Analyst
  3. IT Support Specialist
  4. Cloud Solutions Engineer
  5. Systems Engineer

Program 04

Diploma & Certificate in Web Development

Master the art and science of building modern, responsive web applications. This program covers both front-end design and back-end development using industry-standard frameworks and tools.

Technologies Covered:
  • HTML5, CSS3 & JavaScript (ES6+)
  • React.js & Vue.js (Frontend Frameworks)
  • Node.js & PHP (Backend Development)
  • MySQL & MongoDB (Databases)
  • Git & GitHub (Version Control)
  • UI/UX Design Principles & Figma

Upon graduation, students build a professional portfolio of real-world projects ready to show to employers.
