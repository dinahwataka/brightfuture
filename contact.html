Location

Find Us in Kisumu

We are conveniently located on Oginga Odinga Street in the heart of Kisumu City, easily accessible by public transport.


🚌
By Matatu

Take any CBD-bound matatu and alight at Oginga Odinga stage. We are a 2-minute walk from the stage.

🚗
By Private Car

Ample parking is available within the campus compound. Enter via the main gate on Oginga Odinga Street.

🚶
On Foot

A 5-minute walk from Kisumu Bus Park and 10 minutes from Kisumu Railway Station.
