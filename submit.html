Application Received!

Thank you for applying to BrightFuture Institute of Technology


Your admission application has been successfully submitted. Our admissions team will review your application and get back to you within 5–7 working days.

What Happens Next?

  1. You will receive an email confirmation with your application reference number.
  2. Our admissions team will review your documents and qualifications.
  3. If shortlisted, you will be invited for an orientation and enrollment briefing.
  4. You will receive an official admission letter by email and post.
  5. Complete your fee payment to secure your place in the program.
Need Assistance?

If you have any questions about your application, contact our Admissions Office:
📞 +254 711 111 111 • ✉ admissions@brightfuture.ac.ke
Office hours: Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM — 5:30 PM

Reference: Your application will be filed under the 2025/2026 academic year intake. All correspondence will reference your unique Application ID. BrightFuture Institute is accredited by TVETA® and all qualifications are KNQA® registered.
