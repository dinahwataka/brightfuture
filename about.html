Our History

BrightFuture Institute of Technology was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate educators and technology entrepreneurs who recognized the growing demand for practical, skills-based education in Western Kenya.

Starting with just three programs and 120 students, the institute has grown into one of Kisumu’s most respected technical institutions, with over 2,500 graduates working across Kenya and internationally.

Over the years, we have continuously modernized our curriculum, upgraded our facilities, and forged strong partnerships with industry leaders to ensure our graduates are job-ready from day one.

Key Milestones

  1. 2010 — Institute founded; first intake of 120 students
  2. 2013 — Accredited by the Technical and Vocational Education Training Authority (TVETA)
  3. 2016 — Expanded to 4 academic programs; new ICT lab opened
  4. 2019 — 1,000th graduate milestone celebrated
  5. 2022 — Introduced Web Development program; online learning portal launched
  6. 2024 — Over 2,500 graduates; ranked top 5 institute in Western Kenya
Purpose

Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality technical and vocational education that equips students with the knowledge, skills, and professional values needed to thrive in a rapidly evolving global economy.

We are committed to innovation, inclusivity, and industry alignment in everything we do.

💡 Our Vision

To be the leading institute of technology and innovation in East Africa, recognized for producing graduates who are critical thinkers, ethical leaders, and agents of positive change in society.

We envision a future where every student from Kisumu has access to world-class education.

Accreditation & Partnerships

TVETA — Technical and Vocational Education Training Authority
Fully accredited by TVETA since 2013. Our programs meet all national standards for technical and vocational training.
Kenya National Qualifications Authority (KNQA)
Our diploma and certificate qualifications are registered with KNQA, ensuring they are nationally recognized and portable.
Microsoft Imagine Academy Partner
Students have access to Microsoft certification pathways as part of their IT and Computer Science programs.
Google for Education
BrightFuture is a recognized Google for Education partner, integrating Google Workspace tools across all programs.
